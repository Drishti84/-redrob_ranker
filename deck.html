Feature Engineering
30+ signals extracted per candidate
from raw profile data
🔧 Skill Analysis
Each skill hit scored by proficiency level (beginner→expert: 0.4–1.0×), duration weight (0–6 mo: 0.5×, 6–24: 0.8×, 24+: 1.0×), and endorsement count (up to +20% boost). Covers 14 required skills and 8 nice-to-have skills from the JD.
📈 Career History
Extracted: ai_role_months (total months in AI titles), consulting_only flag (TCS, Infosys, Wipro, Cognizant…), has_product_experience, avg_tenure_months, title_chaser (avg tenure < 18 months).
🎓 Education
4-tier classification: Tier 1 (IIT, IISc, BITS Pilani, NIT Trichy) → Tier 4 (unranked). Scores: 1.0 / 0.75 / 0.45 / 0.20.
📡 Availability (23 redrob_signals)
All 23 platform behavioral signals used:

open_to_work  notice_days  response_rate  interview_rate  offer_rate  github_score  profile_complete  verified  saved_30d  in_india  days_since_active
🔤 Embedding Text
Concatenated profile text for embedding: current title + company + all career roles + skills with durations + education. Structured to emphasise recent, domain-relevant tokens.

Model: all-MiniLM-L6-v2 — 22M params, 384-dim, fast CPU inference, strong multilingual performance.

Key Design Decisions
Three non-obvious choices that
meaningfully improved ranking quality
🚫 Keyword Stuffer Penalty
Problem: A "Marketing Manager at Wipro" who lists every AI skill (FAISS, Qdrant, Transformers) was scoring 0.62 on skill match — above genuine ML engineers.

Fix: If is_non_ai_title AND ai_role_months < 12, apply a 0.40× multiplier on skill score. Score dropped from 0.62 → 0.25. Logic: skills without career evidence are aspirational, not demonstrated.
🍯 Honeypot Detection
Problem: ~65 profiles are intentionally impossible (planted to catch cheaters who include all profiles or who don't validate data).

Detection rules: career date math vs. duration_months differs by >12 months; expert skill listed with 0 months experience; YoE > 5 years beyond total career history; jobs overlapping by >180 days; 5+ expert skills each used <3 months. All 65 detected → excluded from final ranking.
⚡ Full Sweep vs. FAISS Pre-filter
Initial design: FAISS retrieved top 10K by cosine similarity → score those 10K.

Problem found: 5 legitimate AI engineers (AI Engineer at Zoho, NLP Engineer at InMobi…) had low semantic similarity despite strong rule-based scores and were missed entirely by FAISS.

Fix: Score all 100K candidates directly. Scoring 100K takes 1.7s — FAISS saved nothing worth the recall loss. Full sweep = 100% recall.

Why features.jsonl instead of pickle?
Pickle on a 357MB list-of-dicts took 93 minutes to deserialize. JSONL line-by-line loads the same data in 3.7 seconds. Critical fix.
8 / 10
Tech Stack
Minimal, fast, reproducible —
no proprietary APIs or cloud dependencies
🧠 Embeddings
all-MiniLM-L6-v2
22M parameters, 384-dim output, fast CPU inference. Chosen over larger models for the 5-min constraint while retaining strong semantic quality for technical text.
🔢 Similarity
NumPy matrix multiply
Embeddings L2-normalised at precompute time → dot product = cosine similarity. (100K×384) @ (384×1) in 0.04s. No FAISS needed at query time.
⚡ Feature Store
JSONL (line-delimited JSON)
246MB. Loads 100K feature dicts in 3.7s vs 93 minutes for pickle. Standard, human-readable, no serialisation overhead.
🔍 Fuzzy Matching
rapidfuzz
Partial ratio matching for skill names. Catches "HuggingFace" vs "Hugging Face Transformers", "Open Search" vs "OpenSearch" etc.
🖥️ Demo App
Gradio 4 on HuggingFace Spaces
JD text input, adjustable weight sliders, ranked table + Top-10 full reasoning. Deployed at HF Spaces (CPU Basic, free tier).

Summary
What makes this ranker
actually good
🎯
Skill evidence, not lists
Proficiency + duration + endorsements. A 2-month beginner in FAISS scores far lower than a 3-year expert.
🏭
Career trajectory matters
Product company AI experience outweighs consulting firm résumés. Title chasers and non-AI titles penalised.
📞
Hireability signals
A perfect candidate who won't respond is worthless. Response rate, notice period, and active status are first-class signals.
🍯
Trap-aware
65 honeypot profiles detected and removed. Keyword stuffers caught via career-evidence penalty. Ranking is robust.
